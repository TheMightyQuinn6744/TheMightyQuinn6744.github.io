About

My name is Quinn Rodney. I'm a 26 year old aspiring self taught web developer located in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. From the minute I built my first static webpage using basic HTML & CSS, I knew that web development was a passion that I wanted to pursue. The first language I learned was Python, I did this by completing the Python 3 course on codeacademy and working my way through "Python Crash Course: A Hands-on, Project-based Introduction to Programming". After learning Python, I decided to decided to study web development and began learning HTML5, CSS3, and basic JavaScript.

Who Am I? I am a husband & father to a beautiful wife and four year old daughter. I enjoy traveling both domestically and abroad, and just recently returned home after spending a week in Paris! I am a foodie by default and love to cook my own dishes, as well as try out new cuisine when traveling. I enjoy mobile photography, and am always looking for people, places, or things to point my camera at! Lastly, I am a life long gamer. Gaming is a passion for me that was evoked at a very young age. These days I don't game nearly as much as I did in my youth, but I still own both a PS4 & Xbox One.
